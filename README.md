 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive sales analysis dashboard for Blinkit**, built using **Tableau**. The dashboard analyzes sales performance, product demand, and outlet distribution across different outlet sizes, locations, and product categories.

The goal of this project is to convert raw retail data into **actionable insights** that can help businesses understand customer behavior, identify high-performing products, and optimize outlet strategies.

---

## Key Performance Indicators

The dashboard highlights the following important business metrics:

- **Total Sales:** $1.2M
- **Total Items Sold:** 8,523
- **Average Sales per Item:** 141
- **Average Customer Rating:** 3.97 / 5

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Sales Trend by Outlet Establishment
The outlet establishment analysis shows how sales performance evolved over time.

- Sales increased from **78.1K in 2011**
- Reached the **highest peak of 204.5K in 2018**
- Stabilized around **131.5K in 2022**

This suggests a major expansion phase around **2017–2018**.

---

### 2. Top Product Categories

Top-performing product categories based on total sales:

| Item Type | Sales |
|-----------|------|
| Fruits & Vegetables | 178.1K |
| Snack Foods | 175.4K |
| Household | 136K |
| Frozen Foods | 118.6K |
| Dairy | 101.3K |

**Insight:** Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods together contribute a significant share of total revenue.

---

### 3. Fat Content Analysis

The dashboard compares sales between **Regular** and **Low Fat** products.

- **Regular Products:** 776K (~65%)
- **Low Fat Products:** 425K (~35%)

**Insight:** Customers show a stronger preference for regular products.

---

### 4. Outlet Size Performance

| Outlet Size | Sales |
|-------------|------|
| Medium | 507K (~42%) |
| Small | 444K (~37%) |
| Large | 248K (~21%) |

**Insight:** Medium-sized outlets generate the highest revenue.

---

### 5. Outlet Location Analysis

| Location Tier | Sales |
|---------------|------|
| Tier 3 | 472K |
| Tier 2 | 393K |
| Tier 1 | 336K |

**Insight:** Tier 3 cities generate the highest sales, indicating strong demand in smaller markets.

---

### 6. Outlet Type Performance

Supermarkets contribute the largest share of revenue and carry the highest number of products.

- **Total Items in Supermarkets:** 5,577
- **Total Sales Contribution:** ~787K
